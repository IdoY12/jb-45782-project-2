About Crypto World



Crypto World is a project I made while studying Full Stack Web Development at John Bryce College. It shows live coin prices, charts, and lets you keep a small watchlist. I've been around computers my whole life. As a kid I played video games nonstop, and with brothers who are programmers I was always curious about coding. I started learning at 20, and now at 21 I'm already building projects.
